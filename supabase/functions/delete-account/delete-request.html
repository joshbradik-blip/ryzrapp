Data Deletion Request

RYZR by Bradik Enterprises

You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the RYZR app by going to Profile → Delete Account. This will immediately remove your data and sign you out.

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to submit a request by email, use the button below. We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Name, email, age, sex, height, weight, and fitness profile
  • Injury history, goals, and equipment preferences
  • Workout history, sets, reps, weights, and session data
  • AI-generated workout plans and exercise substitutions
Request Deletion by Email
Include the email address associated with your RYZR account in your message. You will receive a confirmation once deletion is complete.
